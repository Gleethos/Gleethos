DOSS - Dungeon Of the Sleepy Stone

In my third semester of my undergrad studies of the U.A.S Technikum I joined a fifth semester project team developing a dungeon crawler game which we were then porting to VR and expanding it's features.
Besides refining my knowledge of the Unity game engine I also learned a great deal about developing for VR, especially the challenges of producing a good user experience or usability in general for such novel hardware has been extremely exciting and educational.

The game is an unfinished product which is currently not in active development.
However me and my team plan on resuming its development in order to eventually publish it on steam.
The game started off as a university project intended as a learning experience and not as a commercial product, we therefore only managed to give it only so much time so far.
